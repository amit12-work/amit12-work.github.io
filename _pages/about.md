With over 14 years of dedicated experience in the IBM i (Power Systems) ecosystem, I specialize in bridging the gap between legacy reliability and modern innovation.

After completing my Master of Computer Applications (MCA) and rigorous industrial training on the AS/400 platform, I began my career delivering high-stakes solutions for the US rental industry. My journey progressed into the manufacturing sector, where I managed and enhanced large-scale, in-house ERP systems for a leading US firm. During this time, I transitioned from development, support and enhancement roles.

Since 2022, I have been partnering with UK-based manufacturing leaders to spearhead complex modernization projects, ensuring their legacy power remains competitive in a digital-first world.
